âœ… 2. Process Queues
OS keeps different queues for different states:

a. Job Queue:
Stores new programs.

Stored in hard disk.

Picked by Long-Term Scheduler.

b. Ready Queue:
Stores processes ready to run.

Stored in main memory (RAM).

Picked by Short-Term Scheduler (CPU Scheduler).

c. Waiting Queue:
Stores processes waiting for some I/O.
